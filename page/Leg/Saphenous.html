Saphenous

Right
Left

Indications

Lacerations, abscesses to medial leg

Contraindications

Infection overlying injection site
Allergy to local anesthetic

Equipment

5-10cc of local anesthetic of choice
22G needle
Cleansing solution
Ultrasound with high-frequency linear transducer>
Ultrasound transducer sterile cover

Preparation

Position

  1. Position the patient supine, elevate and externally rotate the target extremity to expose the medialaspect

Ultrasound

Landmarks

  1. Place the transducer in transverse plane along the medial aspect of the lower extremityapproximately 10cm proximal to the knee (medial femoral condyle)
  2. The saphenous nerve is located deep to the sartorius muscle

Technique

  1. Introduce needle from anterior aspect
  2. In-plane needle visualization
  3. Infiltrate local anesthetic around identified saphenous nerve

Source

Highland Ultrasound
